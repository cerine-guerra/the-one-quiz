## 🎯 What is this?

This isn't your typical personality quiz. It's a **honesty-forcing tool** designed for someone who wants to see clearly — without the filter of charm, chemistry, or wishful thinking.

**30 real-life scenarios** across 6 dimensions:
- ✦ Character & Integrity
- ✦ Mind & Vision  
- ✦ Values & Faith
- ✦ Lifestyle & Energy
- ✦ Relationship Depth
- ⚠️ Red Flag Check

---

## 🔮 How It Works

1. Enter basic info (name, age, hobbies)
2. Answer 30 questions — each with 4 honest choices
3. Get an **instant, unfiltered result** with:
   - Overall score (0-100%)
   - Breakdown by category
   - "Things to watch" section (red flags)
   - Verdict you can trust

Results are automatically emailed to **cerineguerra@gmail.com** — no chasing, no guessing.

The quiz sends results to cerineguerra@gmail.com using EmailJS. If you want to change the destination email:

Go to EmailJS.com

Update the template's "To Email" field

Replace the keys in index.html:

javascript

const EJS_SERVICE = 'your_service_id';
